General Manager - Finance

Location: Chennai

Job Description

  • Work closely with the Senior Executive Leadership team. Continuously communicate, interact and engage with the top management. Give insights, guide, and actively take part in important management decisions.
  • Assess and evaluate the financial performance of the organization with regard to long-term operational goals, budgets, and forecasts. Provide recommendations for both the short-term and long-term growth plans of the organization.
  • Control and evaluate organizations fundraising plans, capital restructuring, and working knowledge of raising capital beyond traditional lines of credit.
  • Create and establish yearly financial objectives that align with the organization's plan for growth and expansion.
  • Develop and maintain a monthly operating budget and annual company operating budget.
  • Prepare and present monthly financial budgeting reports including monthly profit and loss by division, forecast vs. budget by division, and weekly cash flow and funds flow by division.
